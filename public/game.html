About Snake Classic

Snake Classic is one of the most iconic and beloved browser games of all time. Originally popularized on Nokia mobile phones in the late 1990s, this timeless game has been enjoyed by hundreds of millions of people around the world. The concept is beautifully simple: guide a growing snake around the screen, collect food, and avoid hitting the walls or your own tail.

As you eat more food, your snake grows longer, making navigation increasingly difficult and satisfying. How long can you survive before things get too tight? Whether you're playing for the first time or chasing a high score, Snake Classic never gets old.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to control the direction of your snake
  • Guide the snake to eat the food that appears on the screen
  • Each piece of food makes your snake grow longer
  • Avoid running into the walls or your own tail — that ends the game
  • The longer your snake, the higher your score!
